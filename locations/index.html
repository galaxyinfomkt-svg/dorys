Your Trusted Massachusetts Cleaning Partner

For over 21 years, Dory's healthcare sanitation services has been the trusted choice for families and businesses throughout Central and Eastern Massachusetts. From Worcester to the MetroWest area, from Newton to Framingham, we've built our reputation on reliability, attention to detail, and genuine care for every space we clean.

Our service area spans more than 100 communities across Middlesex, Worcester, and Norfolk counties. Whether you're a busy professional in Wellesley needing regular housekeeping, a property manager in Marlborough preparing for new tenants, or an office manager in Cambridge requiring daily janitorial services, we have the expertise and flexibility to meet your needs.

What sets us apart is our commitment to using eco-friendly, hospital-grade cleaning products that are safe for your family, pets, and the environment. Every member of our team is fully insured (up to $2 million in coverage), background-checked, and trained in our proprietary cleaning methods that ensure consistent, thorough results every time.
