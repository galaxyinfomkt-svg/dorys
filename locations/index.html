Your Trusted Massachusetts Healthcare Partner

For over 22 years, Dory's Cleaning Services has been the trusted choice for medical offices, clinics, and healthcare facilities throughout Central and Eastern Massachusetts. From Worcester to the MetroWest area, from Newton to Framingham, we've built our reputation on precision sanitation, infection control protocols, and genuine commitment to patient and staff safety.

Our service area spans more than 100 communities across Middlesex, Worcester, and Norfolk counties. Whether you manage a medical office in Wellesley, oversee an assisted living facility in Marlborough, or direct operations at a clinic in Cambridge, we have the environment-specific expertise and flexibility to meet your sanitation needs.

What sets us apart is our commitment to using EPA-registered hospital-grade disinfectants and following CDC/OSHA guidelines for healthcare facility cleaning services. Every member of our team is fully insured (up to $2 million in coverage), background-checked, and trained in infection control protocols that ensure consistent, compliant results every time.
