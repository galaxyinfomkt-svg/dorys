What Affects Your Healthcare Cleaning Price?

Custom pricing is calculated based on six key factors. Understanding these helps you compare quotes accurately:

📐 Facility Size

Square footage drives base labor hours. Larger facilities have lower per-sq-ft cost due to economies of scale.

📅 Service Frequency

Daily cleaning costs more in absolute dollars but less per visit. Weekly contracts have higher per-visit pricing.

🏥 Facility Type

Surgery centers and dialysis clinics need terminal cleaning. Medical offices need standard infection control.

🔬 Specialty Protocols

ATP testing, EPA List H disinfectants for C. auris, terminal cleaning add to base price.

📋 Documentation Level

Joint Commission and AAAHC accreditation prep includes detailed audit trails — adds 5-10% to base.

🚨 Emergency Response

24/7 biohazard cleanup, COVID outbreak response, and post-flood disinfection priced per incident.
