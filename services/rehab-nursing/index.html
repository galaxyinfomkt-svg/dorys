Rehabilitation centers and skilled nursing facilities require specialized healthcare that prioritizes patient safety, infection prevention, and regulatory compliance. Dorys Janitorial Cleaning Services delivers thorough sanitation using resident-safe, low-toxicity products appropriate for recovering patients and long-term residents.

We understand the unique challenges of patient rooms, therapy areas, dining halls, common spaces, and specialized treatment rooms. Our team provides consistent sanitation that supports infection prevention while maintaining a clean, comfortable healing environment.

With 22 years of clinical experience, our staff is trained to work respectfully around patient schedules and therapy sessions, providing thorough cleaning with minimal disruption to recovery activities.
