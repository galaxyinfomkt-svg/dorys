Why Massachusetts Healthcare Facilities Choose Dorys

With 22 years of experience serving healthcare communities from Boston to Worcester, Framingham to Cambridge, we understand what Massachusetts healthcare administrators and facility managers expect from a professional sanitation partner. Our team is fully licensed (MA HIC #213341) and carries $2 million in insurance coverage, giving you complete peace of mind when you entrust us with your facility.

We've built our reputation on three core principles: rigorous infection control standards, clear compliance documentation, and reliable service you can count on. These values have helped us grow from a small local operation to one of the most trusted healthcare sanitation companies in the state, now serving over 100 cities across Massachusetts.

Medical Office Cleaning and Clinic Sanitation

From small physician practices in Framingham to multi-location clinic networks in Worcester, our medical healthcare environmental services are designed to meet the unique demands of healthcare environments. We use hospital-grade EPA-registered disinfectants and follow CDC-aligned protocols to ensure every exam room, waiting area, and patient contact surface meets the highest sanitation standards.

Our clinic and outpatient facility sanitation covers procedure rooms, recovery areas, instrument processing zones, and all patient-facing surfaces. We understand the critical importance of maintaining a safe, hygienic environment for both patients and staff.

Assisted Living and Infection Control

Our assisted living sanitation services prioritize resident safety with gentle yet thorough cleaning protocols designed for memory care units, resident rooms, dining facilities, and common areas. We train our staff in the specialized needs of senior care environments, including sensitivity to vulnerable populations and adherence to state regulatory requirements.

Our infection control and high-touch disinfection services use targeted protocols to reduce pathogen transmission on the surfaces that matter most. From door handles and light switches to elevator buttons and shared equipment, we apply hospital-grade products with CDC-aligned methods.

Compliance Documentation and Scheduled Programs

Beyond our core sanitation services, we provide structured compliance documentation including sanitation logs, quality audit reports, and scheduled recurring programs. Healthcare facilities trust us for regulatory peace of mind with documented accountability at every visit.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our healthcare sanitation services

What types of healthcare facilities do you serve?

We serve a wide range of healthcare facilities across Massachusetts including medical offices, outpatient clinics, urgent care centers, assisted living facilities, dental offices, ambulatory surgical centers, and memory care units. Our team is trained to meet the specific sanitation requirements of each facility type.

Are your staff trained for healthcare environments?

Yes, all of our sanitation staff receive specialized training in infection control procedures, bloodborne pathogen awareness, proper PPE usage, and CDC/OSHA-aligned sanitation protocols. We maintain ongoing training programs to keep our team current with the latest healthcare sanitation standards.

Do you provide compliance documentation?

Yes, we provide comprehensive compliance documentation including detailed sanitation logs, quality audit reports, and scheduled service records. Our documentation supports your facility's regulatory requirements and provides complete accountability for every visit.

What products do you use?

We use hospital-grade EPA-registered disinfectants that are proven effective against a broad spectrum of pathogens. Our products are selected to meet the specific needs of healthcare environments while ensuring safety for patients, staff, and visitors.

Can you customize a sanitation schedule?

Absolutely! We offer contract-based recurring sanitation programs with guaranteed teams, scheduled visits, and documented accountability. We can customize frequency, timing, and scope to match your facility's specific needs and operational hours.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed (MA HIC #213341) and carry $2,000,000 in liability insurance coverage. This protects your facility, your patients, and our team members. We maintain all required certifications for healthcare environmental services in Massachusetts.
