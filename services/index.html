FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our healthcare services

What types of healthcare facilities do you serve?

We serve medical offices, specialty clinics (including cardiology practices), ambulatory and outpatient facilities, rehabilitation and nursing facilities, and healthcare administrative offices across Massachusetts. Each service plan is tailored to the specific needs and workflow of the facility.

Are your staff trained for clinical environments?

Yes, all of our sanitation staff receive specialized training in infection control procedures, bloodborne pathogen awareness, proper PPE usage, and CDC/OSHA-aligned sanitation protocols. We maintain ongoing training programs to keep our team current with the latest healthcare standards.

Do you provide compliance documentation?

Yes, we provide comprehensive compliance documentation including detailed sanitation logs, quality audit reports, and scheduled service records. Our documentation supports your facility's regulatory requirements and provides complete accountability for every visit.

What products do you use?

We use hospital-grade EPA-registered disinfectants that are proven effective against a broad spectrum of pathogens. Our products are selected to meet the specific needs of clinical environments while ensuring safety for patients, staff, and visitors.

Can you customize a sanitation schedule?

Absolutely! We offer contract-based recurring sanitation programs with guaranteed teams, scheduled visits, and documented accountability. We can customize frequency, timing, and scope to match your facility's specific needs and operational hours.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured in Massachusetts, carrying $2,000,000 in liability insurance coverage. This protects your facility, your patients, and our team members. We maintain all required certifications for healthcare facility cleaning services in Massachusetts.
