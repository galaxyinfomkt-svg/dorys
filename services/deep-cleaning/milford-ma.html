Deep Cleaning FAQ for Milford

Deep cleaning is more intensive and thorough. It includes cleaning areas often missed in regular cleaning like inside cabinets, behind appliances, baseboards, and detailed scrubbing of bathrooms and kitchens.

Deep cleaning typically takes 4-8 hours depending on the size of your home or business and its current condition. Larger spaces or very dirty areas may require additional time.

We recommend deep cleaning 2-4 times per year as a supplement to regular cleaning services. It's also ideal before holidays, special events, or when moving.
