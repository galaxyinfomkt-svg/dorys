## IMPORTANT NOTES

### DO:
- Focus on local, relevant links
- Prioritize quality over quantity
- Maintain NAP consistency everywhere
- Build relationships, not just links

### DON'T:
- Buy links or use PBNs
- Submit to spammy directories
- Use exact-match anchor text excessively
- Neglect link maintenance
