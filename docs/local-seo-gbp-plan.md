Service-area B2B healthcare cleaning company, Marlborough MA. This is the **#1
lead lever** for a local service business (the Google Maps "local pack" + reviews
drive more calls than any on-site change). All of this is **off-site** — the
agency (Galaxy IT) executes it in Google Business Profile, directories and the
review tools. The website is already strong; this is what's missing.

**First task:** audit current NAP everywhere it appears (GBP, Yelp, Facebook, BBB, old directories) with BrightLocal / Whitespark / Moz Local. **Fix duplicates and inconsistencies BEFORE adding new citations** — compounding errors hurt.

## 1. Google Business Profile — setup & optimization

Configure as a **Service-Area Business (SAB)**, not a storefront.

- [ ] **Hide the street address**, set **service areas** (you don't serve walk-ins). Add the top regions: Middlesex, Worcester, Suffolk, Norfolk, Essex counties + name your densest cities (Marlborough, Framingham, Worcester, Newton, Natick, Hudson, Worcester, Boston, Quincy, Lowell…). GBP allows ~20 service areas — pick your highest-value cities.
- [ ] **Primary category:** `Commercial cleaning service`
- [ ] **Secondary categories:** `Janitorial service`, `Building cleaning service`, `Industrial cleaning service`, `Disinfecting service` (add the ones GBP offers). Categories are the single biggest GBP ranking factor after proximity/reviews.
- [ ] **Description (750 chars, keywords in first 100):** lead with "Commercial healthcare facility cleaning in Massachusetts — CDC/OSHA-compliant environmental services for medical offices, clinics, outpatient & surgery centers, rehab/nursing and assisted-living facilities. 22+ years clinical experience, $2M insured, MA HIC #213341. Free facility assessment, 24-hour response."
- [ ] **Services:** add each service as a GBP service item with a short description: Medical Office Cleaning, Specialty Clinic Sanitation, Ambulatory/Outpatient Cleaning, Surgery Center Terminal Cleaning, Rehab & Nursing Cleaning, Assisted Living Cleaning, Infection Control / High-Touch Disinfection, ATP Testing, COVID/Biohazard Disinfection, 24/7 Emergency.
- [ ] **Attributes:** "Identifies as women-owned", "Identifies as Black-owned" (matches the supplier-diversity positioning), "Online estimates", "Onsite services".
- [ ] **Hours:** Mon–Sat 5:00 AM–7:00 PM (match the site); add 24/7 emergency note in description.
- [ ] **Products/Highlights:** add the free facility assessment as a featured item.
- [ ] **Booking/Quote link:** link the website quote/contact page.

## 3. Reviews — the biggest movable lever (currently 4.7 / 10 reviews)

> Volume + recency + your **responses** move local rank more than almost anything. 10 reviews is low for the category; competitors likely have 30–100+.

- [ ] **Ask every client** for a Google review after each completed job. Send the **GBP short review link** by text/email the same day.
- [ ] Target **1–2 new reviews per week** (sustained velocity beats a one-time burst).
- [ ] **Respond to 100% of reviews** within 24–48h — thank by name, reference the facility type (HIPAA-safe, no patient/client confidential detail), restate a keyword ("medical office cleaning in Framingham").
- [ ] Seed a simple ask script: *"If we kept your facility audit-ready this month, a 30-second Google review really helps other administrators find us."*
- [ ] Never gate or buy reviews (Google penalizes).

## 5. GBP Posts — weekly cadence

Post **weekly** (Posts expire/lose weight after ~7 days). Rotate types:

- **Offer/CTA:** "Free facility assessment + compliance walkthrough — book this week."
- **Update:** a service spotlight (e.g., "Terminal cleaning for surgery centers").
- **Educational:** a tip ("What ATP testing proves about your high-touch surfaces") — links to the matching site page/blog.
- **Proof:** a review quote or a before/after.

Each post: 1 image, 1 keyword phrase, 1 clear CTA (Call / Book), link to the relevant site page.
