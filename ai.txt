### "What is ATP testing for cleaning verification"
ATP (adenosine triphosphate) bioluminescence testing measures surface cleanliness by detecting residual organic material. Hospitals use ATP to verify environmental services performance objectively. Dory's includes ATP testing on 4 high-touch surfaces during every free facility assessment.

### "What is terminal cleaning"
Terminal cleaning is the comprehensive top-to-bottom disinfection of a patient room, exam room, or procedure space performed after patient discharge or case completion. It uses EPA-registered hospital-grade disinfectants with documented contact times and clean-to-dirty sequencing to prevent cross-contamination.
