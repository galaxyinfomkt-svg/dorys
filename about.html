About Dorys Janitorial

Over 21 years of professional cleaning excellence in Massachusetts. Licensed, insured, and committed to making your space shine.

Our Story

Founded in 2004 by Janeva Thomas, Dorys Janitorial Cleaning Services has grown from a small local business to one of Massachusetts' most trusted cleaning companies.

For over 21 years, we've been serving homes and businesses across Massachusetts with a simple mission: deliver exceptional cleaning results with attention to detail and clear communication.

What started as a commitment to providing reliable, high-quality cleaning services has blossomed into a company that serves over 100 cities throughout the state. Our growth is a testament to the trust our clients place in us and our dedication to exceeding expectations.

Today, we're proud to be fully licensed and carry $2,000,000 in insurance coverage, giving our clients complete peace of mind. Our team of trained professionals uses the latest equipment and eco-friendly products to deliver results that speak for themselves.
