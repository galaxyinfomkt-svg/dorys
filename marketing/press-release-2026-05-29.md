## FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

### Dory's Cleaning Services Expands Clinical Environmental Cleaning to 296 Massachusetts Cities

**Marlborough, MA — May 29, 2026** — Dory's Cleaning Services Inc., the Marlborough-based commercial cleaning firm founded in 2003, today announced statewide expansion of its specialized healthcare facility environmental services across 296 Massachusetts cities. The 22-year-old company, founded and led by Jeneva Thomas, exclusively serves medical offices, specialty clinics, ambulatory and outpatient facilities, rehabilitation and nursing centers, and healthcare administrative offices — bringing hospital-grade infection control standards to non-hospital healthcare settings throughout the Commonwealth.

The expansion responds to growing demand from Massachusetts outpatient facilities, ambulatory surgery centers, dental practices, and specialty clinics for environmental services providers with documented clinical credentials rather than general janitorial vendors. Recent Joint Commission and CMS audits across the state have intensified scrutiny of environmental cleaning documentation, OSHA Bloodborne Pathogens compliance, and EPA-registered disinfectant matrices.

"Outpatient and specialty clinic administrators in Massachusetts are tired of explaining infection control standards to vendors who do not understand them," said Jeneva Thomas, Founder and CEO of Dory's Cleaning Services Inc. "Our team certifies every staff member in OSHA Bloodborne Pathogens (29 CFR 1910.1030), trains them in HIPAA-aware environmental services, and provides written compliance documentation for accreditation surveys. That is the baseline for clinical cleaning — and it should not be unusual."

**Key facility credentials:**

- **MA HIC License #213341** — Massachusetts Home Improvement Contractor
- **$2,000,000 general liability insurance** coverage
- **OSHA Bloodborne Pathogens** training documentation maintained per 29 CFR 1910.1030
- **EPA-registered hospital-grade disinfectants** including EPA List N and List K (sporicidal) products
- **ATP bioluminescence verification testing** — same surface verification methodology used by major teaching hospitals
- **4.7 / 5.0 Google rating** (10 verified reviews) — https://doryscleaningservices.com/reviews

**Service categories include:**

- Medical office cleaning (physician practices, internal medicine, OB/GYN, pediatrics, family medicine)
- Specialty clinic sanitation (cardiology, dermatology, dental, urgent care, orthopedics)
- Ambulatory and outpatient facility cleaning (ambulatory surgery centers, infusion centers, dialysis)
- Rehabilitation and nursing facility environmental services (long-term care, skilled nursing, memory care, assisted living)
- Healthcare administrative office cleaning (medical billing, hospital headquarters, healthcare admin spaces)

Dory's Cleaning Services does NOT provide residential or home cleaning. All contracts are commercial healthcare facility-only.
