Cleaning Newton & Brookline's Luxury Homes: Expert Tips

October 15, 2024 6 min read

Newton and Brookline are home to some of Greater Boston's most prestigious residences. From historic mansions to sleek modern homes, these properties demand specialized cleaning knowledge and careful attention to high-end finishes.

Neighborhoods We Serve

Newton

  • Chestnut Hill: Grand estates, often with pools and extensive grounds
  • Newton Centre: Mix of historic Victorians and updated homes
  • Waban: Family-friendly with large colonial homes
  • West Newton Hill: Prestigious, tree-lined streets

Brookline

  • Fisher Hill: Stately mansions with significant square footage
  • Pill Hill: Historic district with preserved architecture
  • Chestnut Hill (Brookline): Luxury condos and single-family homes
  • Coolidge Corner area: Mix of apartments and townhomes

Luxury Finish Care

Marble and Natural Stone

  • Use pH-neutral cleaners only
  • Never use vinegar or acidic products
  • Seal annually
  • Blot spills immediately
  • Use coasters and trivets

Designer Kitchen Appliances

  • Wolf, Sub-Zero, Miele, La Cornue require specific care
  • Follow manufacturer guidelines
  • Stainless steel: clean with grain
  • Avoid abrasives on any surface

Custom Millwork

  • Dust with soft microfiber
  • Know the finish type (lacquer, paint, stain)
  • Use appropriate products for each finish
  • Avoid water on detailed carvings

Smart Home Considerations

Many Newton and Brookline homes feature smart technology:

  • Clean touch panels gently with appropriate wipes
  • Know how to silence alarms before cleaning
  • Be mindful of motion sensors
  • Don't unplug or move smart devices
  • Coordinate with home automation systems

Managing Large Properties

Efficiency Strategies

  • Zone cleaning approach for homes 4,000+ sq ft
  • Team cleaning for comprehensive service
  • Supply caddies on each floor
  • Detailed checklists for consistency

Commonly Forgotten Areas

  • Third floor spaces
  • Finished basements with theaters/gyms
  • Multiple garage bays
  • Mudrooms and back entries
  • Staff quarters if applicable
